Firm Overview

The People's Firm, LP

The People's Firm, LP was founded by Prior Service Veteran Brandon Williams, JD. After Serving as a Department of Veterans Affairs Lead Judge Adjudicator for 5 years, he realized that there needed to be a change in the way that the VA Disability Claims Process was being performed.

In April 2022, it was decided that he would take his prior knowledge of the claims process and start The People's Firm, LP with the sole intent of helping Prior Service Veterans get the help they need and deserve.
